HazardsAI Project Details

"As someone forced to evacuate due to wildfires this year, and floods a couple years ago, I've always wanted a more accurate web app that could inform me about upcoming disasters better than the news. There have been many situations where the news warnings would not be right, and HazardsAI solves this problem by using realtime evidence. HazardsAI is made up of 3 parts.

  • 1. Earthquake Magnitude linear regression model

    • The project goes one step further to see if there are aftershocks leading to landslides.

  • 2. Droughts and Fire Image Classifier and Random Forest Classigier

    • Next, the drought and fire machine learning detection feature, it uses an image classifier and random forest classifier to detect famine and wildfires.

  • Floods Random Forest Classifier

    • The last part is another random forest classifier to predict floods.

Natural disasters lead to millions of injuries and fatalities per year. HazardsAI aim to minimize this by detecting catastrophese ahead of time that may lead to large casualties. Machine learning is the best way to do this.
