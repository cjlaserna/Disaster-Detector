The Project

The goal

Natural GeoWarns have led to several injuries and fatalities per year. I wanted to minimize this by detecting several dangerous diseases that have had a large impact on the world. In order to do this we have used machine learning and deep learning.

Get Started

Why GeoWarn

As someone forced to evacuate due to several wildfires this year and floods a couple years ago, I wish there was always an app that could inform me better and more accurately than the news. Many atimes the news warnings would not be right, and I wished that there would be some app that could accurately figure out if a GeoWarn is occuring with realtime evidence. This led me to the creation of GeoWarn. Geo Warn is made up of 3 parts. The first part is a linear regression model that detects magnitudes of earthquakes. The project goes one step further to see if there are aftershocks of landslides. Next is the drought and fire machine learning detection feature. It uses an image classifier and random forest classifier to detect famine and wildfires. The last part is another random forest classifier to predict floods.
